principles

  • Deal with the mess. It's just code.
  • Intensity halves at every layer of the hierarchy. If you want more of it, start at the top.
  • You haven't found the root cause until you've found the exact line of code.
  • There are two ways to force culture change: slowly boil the frog or burn the house down. Both work. Pick one.
  • Identify what makes a competitor serious. Safely ignore the rest.
  • Most of your users haven't arrived yet. Keeping that in mind makes tough choices easier.
  • Default to horizontal scaling. Start with at least two instances.
  • Just look at it. Just think about it. Just talk about it.
  • Sometimes, you need to thread the needle.
  • Raise (salaries) without asking and you'll be able to ask without raising.
